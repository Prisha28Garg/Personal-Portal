My time of arrival was 8:56 A.M., my weight was, 3.1kg and my height was, 52cm. I have a younger brother named Pratim Garg. I was the 1st child in my house. My name, Prisha, is a Hindu name and means “God’s Gift” and my lucky number is 8. My parents had a few names planned, like in case of a boy, they would have named me Vihaan. My mom wrote few lines herself for me, which she wrote in the invitations: “A sweet little angel came knocking on the door, and filled our lives with happiness, joy and much much more. My words of Wisdom

Being unique is better than being perfect. My parents have always inspired me to work hard, never give up and have a balanced life. Even if you fail, never give up because F.A.I.L means "The first attempt in learning." When I talk about myself, I love to explore new things. I keep trying new things and I always feel, "We should keep practicing until we get good at it!". When we all try different things, we usually don't get the hang of it on the 1st go, but if we give up and don't try it over again, we will never learn anything new. and that's what happened to me. After a class on HTML, I told my mom that text coding is hard and I couldn't understand. I got a project related to it and my teacher said I should try my best and give it a shot and if I needed guidance, she's always available. Then, my mom said, "Keep trying and if you still don't get it then, ask for guidance." and that's what I did. I practiced and practiced and now I can do it comfortably. I recently overcame this challenge. Though, my teachers and my parents are my backbone and help me climb my ladder. The key is always to 'Never give up, keep trying and if you feel low, then ask for guidance.' and that's what I have learned in the past couple of years.
